What We Do

Fundamental research towards Affordable, Green, Efficient, and Decentralised AI

The Core Problem

Modern AI systems demand enormous computational resources, centralised infrastructure, and significant energy consumption. This creates barriers to access, raises environmental concerns, and concentrates capability in the hands of a few.

Our research asks: how do we make AI fundamentally more affordable, greener, more efficient, and truly decentralised?
